Here are some tricks to improve memory:

* You remember:     
… 20% of what you read.    
… 30% of what you hear.    
… 40% of what you see.    
… 50% of what you say.    
… 60% of what you do.     
… 90% of what you read, hear, see, say and do.     
* Create diagrams and mind maps.
* Think out loud.
